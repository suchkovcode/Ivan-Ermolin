Story

MOLOTOV — This is a self -sufficient brand, which combines the explosive character and harsh disposition of streets with a deeply meaningful approach to creating clothes.

Since 2015, we have successfully support this balance, integrating the interests of local subcultures with the most relevant trends.

Philosophy

We are constantly working on the convenience and functionality of each thing, thinking over everything to the smallest detail.

Passion for experiments and seven years of experience allowed us to create a stylish, affordable and at the same time quality clothes.

Today, Molotov is not just a brand, it is a lifestay and belonging to a special culture.An explosive formula that was lit in the hearts of people, already in more than 50 cities of Russia.

We are inspired by people and constantly communicate with our community.Fidback allows you to create products relevant here and now, and limited parties and recognizable design make collections unique and original.
